Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of software engineers and entrepreneurs in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into one of the world’s largest and most influential technology conferences, drawing innovators from across the globe to share ideas, inspire change, and shape the future.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower creativity and collaboration within the global tech community. We strive to foster innovation, promote sustainable technology development, and bridge the gap between emerging startups and established leaders.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Renowned computer scientist and pioneer in algorithmic thinking, Dr. Lovelace inspired the theme of TechCon 2019, “Coding the Future,” emphasizing the intersection of logic and creativity in programming.

Elon Musk

Visionary entrepreneur and innovator, Elon Musk has shared insights on sustainable technology, space exploration, and the power of bold innovation during TechCon 2021.

Dr. Fei-Fei Li

As a leading AI researcher, Dr. Fei-Fei Li has been instrumental in advancing human-centered artificial intelligence and promoting diversity in STEM, inspiring thousands at TechCon 2022.
